Ice Cream Model

On my path to creating photorealistic renders, I also made an ice cream model that is slightly more complex than the pawn. Using the Eevee engine for the render again, the model uses textures and materials for the cone and ice cream in order to create an appealing-looking model. In addition to this, I used an HDRI to improve the lighting of the scene and of the overall render. The ice cream has 1,278 faces and 2,612 triangles.
