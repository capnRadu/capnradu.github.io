Interior Model

One of the many things that I wish to learn to model in Blender are interiors / archirectural modeling. For this, I tried to experiment by creating a room and filling it with models, textures and materials, and using realistic lighting. I modeled a small room with walls, and searched on Poliigon and Poly Haven textures and materials for the walls and floor, and models to fill the room with. Furthermore, I used Cycles to render the final image.
