Donut Animation

The donut model is the first model I created when I started with Blender. I followed Blender Guru's tutorial because I saw a lot of beginners starting with this tutorial and showcasing it on their progression videos. Overall, I thought it was a very comprehensive tutorial, going through important mechanics that Blender has to offer and which beginners should know about.

Reaching the middle part of the tutorial, I started struggling a bit to understand everything that was presented to me, but managed to complete it and finish the render. As a complete novice, it gave me a broader understanding of Blender, its functions, and how many creative and astonishing animations / models you can develop by yourself.
