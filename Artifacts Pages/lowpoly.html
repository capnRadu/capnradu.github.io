Low Poly World Model

When I started to learn Blender, I wanted to create a low poly model, because I really liked those types of designs and it seemed a good fit for a learning roadmap. I kept postponing this because I felt unmotivated, but eventually did it, and the result turned out great. I used Cycles for the final render, modifiers for the river, and a lot of duplicating for the models that are present in the scene.
